About Us

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Denora Boone Denora M. Boone is the CEO of Anointed Inspirations Publishing, LLC as well as the author of thirty independent novels. Denora grew up in Milledgeville, Ga and began penning stories for fun during her middle and high school years. She didn't know that her creativity would lead to over twenty-five independent #1 bestsellers.

Now stepping out into another area of writing, Denora has become a rising screenwriter in the film and television industry. She has created her first web series titled SHE the Series, that follows the life of a young girl following in the steps of her father in becoming one of the most feared but respected women in the world. SHE can be watched on She The Series.

Denora has also written for the highly popular Youtube web series Project Heat Atlanta and looks forward to joining and creating other productions in the near future.

Because of her great ability to create vivid and captivating stories, Denora is the recipient of the 2015 AAMBC Literary Award for Christian Fiction Author of the Year and nominated for the same award for both 2016 and 2017. She also holds the 2017 MBP Award for Christian Fiction Author of the year, and nominated for the Author Academy Awards 2018 for Best Religious Book for Mama Raised Me, Her Demons Changed Me.

Denora resides with her husband Byron of seventeen years and their four children; Jalen (17), Elijah (16), Mekiyah (14), and Isaiah (13).
